What is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the simple but powerful practice of bringing your full attention to the present moment, without judging or trying to change it.

By cultivating mindfulness, you can better understand your thoughts and emotions, reduce stress, and improve your overall well-being.

Key benefits:

  • Reduce anxiety and stress
  • Improve concentration and focus
  • Enhance emotional regulation
  • Develop self-compassion and kindness
  • Feel more calm and grounded

Guided Body Scan Meditation

Relax your body and slowly bring your attention to each part, noticing sensations without judgment.

  1. Sit or lie down comfortably and close your eyes.
  2. Take a deep breath and notice your feet. Are they warm, cold, tense, or relaxed?
  3. Move your attention up to your calves and knees, then thighs and hips.
  4. Notice your abdomen and chest rising and falling with breath.
  5. Scan your shoulders, arms, hands, neck, and head.
  6. If your mind wanders, gently bring it back to the sensations in your body.
  7. Finish with a few deep breaths and open your eyes slowly.

Daily Mindfulness Tips

  • Start your day with a few deep breaths to set an intention.
  • Try to notice the small details during everyday activities like eating or walking.
  • Use reminders (alarms or sticky notes) to pause and breathe throughout your day.
