Understanding Grief

Grief is a natural response to loss. It's important to know that there's no "right" way to grieve, and everyone's journey is unique.

  • 1Denial: "This can't be happening"
  • 2Anger: Feeling frustrated or upset
  • 3Bargaining: "What if..." thoughts
  • 4Depression: Deep sadness and withdrawal
  • 5Acceptance: Finding peace with the loss

Remember: These stages don't happen in order, and you may experience them multiple times.
