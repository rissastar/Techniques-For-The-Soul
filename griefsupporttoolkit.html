Understanding Grief

Grief is a natural response to loss. It affects everyone differently and there is no right or wrong way to grieve.

  • Allow yourself to feel your emotions fully.
  • Seek support from trusted friends or professionals.
  • Practice self-care and patience.

Remember, healing takes time.

Guided Breathing Exercise

Slowly breathe in for 4 seconds...

Hold your breath for 4 seconds...

Exhale gently for 4 seconds...

Repeat this cycle a few times to relax.
