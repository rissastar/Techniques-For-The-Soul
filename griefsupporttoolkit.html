💬 What is Grief?

Grief is a natural response to loss. It’s different for everyone and can affect your thoughts, feelings, and body. There’s no “right” way to grieve — just your way.

🛠️ Coping Strategies

Breathe deeply for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4.
Write a letter to the person or thing you’ve lost.
Walk outside and notice 5 things you can see, hear, feel.
Say: “It’s okay to not be okay.” Repeat it kindly.

🧡 You're Not Alone

Whether your loss is fresh or from long ago, you are not alone. Let others in, be kind to yourself, and take one breath at a time. Healing doesn’t mean forgetting. It means remembering with love.
