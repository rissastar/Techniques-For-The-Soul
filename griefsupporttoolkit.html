üìò Grief Support Resources

Explore these free resources for support and community:

  • MyGrief.ca - Online resource offering modules on various aspects of grief.
  • Healing Hearts Canada - Free grief support groups available across Canada.
  • GriefShare - Grief recovery support groups with a network of local groups.
  • My Grief Angels - Virtual grief support groups by type of loss.
  • Lumara Society - Virtual grief support groups available across Canada.
  • Nova Scotia Health - Palliative Care Bereavement Program - Offers grief support services including counseling and support groups.
