Use these prompts:

  • What are early warning signs? (e.g. racing thoughts)
  • What healthy actions help? (e.g. walk, call)
  • Who can I reach out to? (include phone numbers)
  • Where can I go if unsafe? (e.g. ER)
  • What steps keep my environment safe?
