    Self-Care Toolbox

    Use these strategies when you feel stressed or triggered.

    • Breathing Exercise: Try 4-7-8 breathing: inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, exhale for 8.
    • Grounding Technique: Identify 5 things you see, 4 things you feel, 3 things you hear, 2 things you smell, 1 thing you taste.
    • Physical Activity: Go for a walk, stretch, or dance to your favorite song.
    • Creative Outlet: Write, draw, or listen to music that uplifts you.
    • Reach Out: Call or text a trusted friend or support person.
    • Journaling: Write about your feelings and thoughts without judgment.
    • Positive Affirmations: Repeat encouraging phrases like "I am strong" or "I can get through this."
