If you feel overwhelmed, try one or more of these steps:

  • Call or text a crisis line: 988 (US/Canada Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • Practice the 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding exercise below
  • Use a sensory tool: hold an ice cube, sniff essential oils, or wrap yourself in a soft blanket
  • Go to a safe and comfortable space
  • Listen to calming music or sounds
  • Reach out to someone you trust
  • Repeat to yourself: "This moment will pass."
